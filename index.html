My Favorite Albums

folklore

by Taylor Swift

folklore is an indie-folk album released by Taylor Swift in 2020. It has been my favorite album of all time since I first listened to it in 2022. My favorite songs are illicit affairs and hoax.

Punisher

by Phoebe Bridgers

Punisher is an indie-folk album released by Phoebe Bridgers in 2020. I first listened to it in 2022 and it quickly became one of my favorites. My favorite songs on the album are Moon Song and I Know the End.

Be The Cowboy

by Mitski

Be The Cowboy is an indie-rock album released by Mitski in 2018. This was the first album from Mitski that I ever listened to and it has been one of my all-time favorites for many years now. My favorite songs are Two Slow Dancers and Pink in the Night.
